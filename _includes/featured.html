Great Features

Usability

A simple pdf and epub reader with bookmark, search, and speed read capabilities

Customization Effect

Sometimes the simplest things are the hardest to find. Reading Hacker allows you to change font color, enable or disable paragraph view or left and right text view, and download a variety of fonts from google fonts!

Copy And Paste Text

Sometimes you need simple functionality. Besides loading book, the app also has a place where text can be copy and pasted!
